Where to go in Bosnia & Herzegovina

From the bazaars of Sarajevo to the bridge at Mostar and the emerald rivers of the north west.

Sarajevo

The capital where East meets West, home to the Baščaršija bazaar, a deep coffee culture and history at every turn.

Mostar

The iconic Stari Most bridge, the turquoise Neretva river and a beautifully preserved Ottoman old town.

Jajce

A waterfall in the centre of town, watched over by a medieval fortress.

Travnik

An Ottoman vizier town with a colourful mosque and a gateway to highland nature.

Blagaj

A dervish monastery at the foot of a cliff, where the Buna river rises from the rock.

Una National Park

Emerald rivers, waterfalls and rafting near Bihać, in Bosnia's wild north west.
